WTF, a garage?

May 15, 2021

Some people call their sites homepage, others blog, some fancy folks call it digital garden.

In my case I call it “Garage”.

To me, a homepage, a blog, or a digital garden evokes some kind of order, commitment, or maintanance. While a garage is this place where you put the stuff you like, even if it is out of fashion, and at some point a friend might be looking at stuffs in your garage and find something interesting.
