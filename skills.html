  • Functional programming

    When it comes to programming, programming with a functional style is nearly a must, in my opinion. It's safer and easier to develop with this style, meaning that there is a big cost reduction in the development cycle.

    When it comes to speed, I prefer to use C++ or Rust, as they are the standard right now, with Rust emerging. Libraries like Boost are a must for me when it comes to utility as they provide extra functionality with no speed cost.
